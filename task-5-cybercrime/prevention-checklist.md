# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist for College Students

## General Cybersecurity
1. Use strong passwords with 12+ characters including letters, numbers,
   and symbols. Never reuse passwords across platforms.
2. Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) on all important accounts
   including email, social media, and banking apps.
3. Never click on links in unknown emails or SMS messages — verify the
   sender before taking any action.
4. Keep your phone's operating system and apps updated regularly to patch
   security vulnerabilities.
5. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or logging into personal accounts.
   Use a VPN if necessary.
6. Never download apps from unknown sources — only use Google Play Store
   or Apple App Store.

## Financial / UPI Safety
7. Remember: entering your UPI PIN always sends money — it never receives it.
   Never enter your PIN for an incoming payment request.
8. Never scan a QR code sent by a stranger claiming to send you money.
   Legitimate senders do not need you to scan anything to receive payment.

## Reporting
9. If you are a victim of cybercrime, report immediately at
   cybercrime.gov.in or call the National Cyber Crime Helpline 1930
   (available 24×7 across India).
