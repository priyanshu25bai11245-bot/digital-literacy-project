# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

## ✅ Do's
1. Keep your LinkedIn profile updated with your education and skills
2. Think carefully before posting — ask yourself if it could affect your reputation
3. Give proper credit when sharing someone else's content or work
4. Use professional language when engaging with faculty or industry professionals
5. Follow and engage with content relevant to your field of study

## ❌ Don'ts
1. Don't share personal information like phone number, address, or location publicly
2. Don't post or share unverified news, rumours, or misinformation
3. Don't engage in or tolerate cyberbullying, hate speech, or offensive comments
4. Don't use the same password across multiple social media platforms
5. Don't post content under the influence of strong emotions — always pause and reflect first
